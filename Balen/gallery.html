Why EDM?

EDM (Exclusive Donation Method) is introduced to help support and maintain the server. Running a FiveM server involves multiple ongoing costs, including: purchasing high-quality scripts, renting powerful VPS hosting, paying for custom development, and ensuring smooth performance and security for all players.

By contributing through EDM, you're helping us keep the server alive, active, and ever-improving. Every contribution directly benefits the community.
